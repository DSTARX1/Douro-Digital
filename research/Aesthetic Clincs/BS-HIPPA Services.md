## HIPAA \& PHI HANDLING PRINCIPLES

**How We Protect Client Data:**

- We treat any combination of name, contact details, and appointment information tied to an identifiable person as PHI in med spa/health settings
- When our services create, receive, maintain, or transmit PHI, we operate as a **Business Associate** and sign BAAs with clients and use only BAA-backed vendors
- We design all automation flows so that:
    - PHI remains inside the client's existing HIPAA-compliant EMR/booking/messaging systems
    - Our AI components run on HIPAA-capable infrastructure with encryption at rest and in transit, access controls, and audit logging
    - Access to PHI is restricted to authorized personnel only via role-based controls
- We instruct clients and our staff to **NEVER paste PHI into consumer AI tools or non-BAA systems**
